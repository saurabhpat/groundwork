# Groundwork: 3-Minute Demo Script (6-Screen Journey)

**Target Duration:** < 3 minutes (~355 spoken words)
**Visual Flow:** Follows the 6 core application screens in precise chronological order.

---

### [0:00 - 0:25] Screen 1: The Proposition (Landing)
**Visual Reference:** `images/landing.png`
- Show the bold landing page text: "Talk to AI before talking to the real person."
- Smooth scroll down to the Call to Action.

**Audio (Voiceover):**
"Eighty-four percent of professionals rely purely on mental rehearsal before a high-stakes conversation—and then freeze when things don't go perfectly to plan. Mental practice isn't enough to build real conversational muscle. Welcome to Groundwork, where you practice those exact, high-stakes conversations out loud *before* you ever walk into the room."

---

### [0:25 - 0:55] Screen 2: Setting the Scene (Onboarding)
**Visual Reference:** `images/onboarding.png`
- Show the conversational or structured onboarding flow.
- A user types a quick context, like preparing to challenge a defensive manager.

**Audio (Voiceover):**
"To make the practice truly valuable, it can't be generic. Groundwork begins with a swift onboarding process where you lay out your specific scenario, your goals, and the communication blockers you are facing. The system acts as an analyst, synthesizing your exact context so the AI understands exactly what's on the line for you."

---

### [0:55 - 1:25] Screen 3: The Prep (Coaching Brief)
**Visual Reference:** `images/brief.png`
- Focus on the synthesized summary and the "Intensity" sliders being adjusted before starting.

**Audio (Voiceover):**
"Before we simulate the conversation, Groundwork provides a personalized Pre-Simulation Brief. This breaks down the anticipated anatomy of your meeting. Because 'Power Dynamics Anxiety' is one of the highest barriers to effective communication, you also configure the simulation here. You manually adjust the stress level and your counterpart's defensiveness so it permanently mirrors the real-world pressure you expect."

---

### [1:25 - 2:05] Screen 4: Active Practice (Simulation)
**Visual Reference:** `images/simulation.png`
- The AI Avatar/Voice speaks a challenging opening line.
- The microphone pulses as the user answers out loud.
- A "Live Coach" suggestion pops up in the UI.

**Audio (Voiceover):**
"Now you enter the simulation. Groundwork forces you to speak out loud, converting passive thought into active practice. Powered by ultra-low latency models, your AI counterpart responds dynamically. It actively pushes back and asks unexpected questions—replicating exactly what derails most professionals. 

If you stumble, you aren't alone. Groundwork's Live Coach provides subtle, real-time tactical adjustments in the margins, helping you navigate the objection and stay on track without ever breaking character."

---

### [2:05 - 2:35] Screen 5: The Analysis (Feedback)
**Visual Reference:** `images/feedback.png`
- Highlight the "Hesitation Markers" and "Stronger Alternative" phrasing suggestions in the UI.

**Audio (Voiceover):**
"When the session is over, the familiar cycle of post-conversation regret is replaced by a structured, analytical debrief. Groundwork's Feedback Engine analyzes your transcript instantly. It highlights your hesitation markers, shows you what actually landed well, and crucially—it provides clearer, stronger phrasing alternatives. You get the exact words to improve your pitch, and the immediate option to rerun the simulation."

---

### [2:35 - 3:00] Screen 6: Tracking Growth (Profile)
**Visual Reference:** `images/profile.png`
- Display the user's progress dashboard, showing cumulative simulation history and past practice attempts.
- Fade out to the Groundwork logo.

**Audio (Voiceover):**
"Finally, every practice session is logged in your personal Analytical Profile. Over time, Groundwork tracks your skill acquisition and conversational confidence. You don't have to leave your career opportunities on the table. Build true muscle memory here, so you can walk confidently into every real-life meeting. This is Groundwork."
